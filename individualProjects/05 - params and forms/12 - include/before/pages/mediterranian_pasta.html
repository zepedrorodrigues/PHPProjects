Mediterranean Marvel Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 400g Penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Prepare the Pasta

  1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook the Penne pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

Prepare the Toppings

  1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until they begin to soften.

Combine Ingredients

  1. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and mix well.
  2. Add the sliced Kalamata olives and sun-dried tomatoes to the skillet and mix again.

Add Final Touches

  1. Remove from heat and add the crumbled feta cheese, torn basil leaves, and pine nuts.
  2. Stir to combine, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
