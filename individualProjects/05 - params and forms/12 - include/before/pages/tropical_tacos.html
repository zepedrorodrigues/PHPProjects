Tropical Tango Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 lb grilled chicken, thinly sliced
  • 1 mango, diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

Prepare the Tropical Salsa

  1. In a bowl, combine the diced mango, avocado, chopped red onion, jalapeño, and cilantro.
  2. Add the juice of one lime and mix well. Season with salt and pepper.

Prepare the Chicken

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the thinly sliced grilled chicken to the skillet.
  3. Sauté until heated through. Add salt, pepper, and the juice of one lime.

Assemble the Tacos

  1. Heat the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave for a few seconds until pliable.
  2. Place a portion of the heated chicken on each tortilla.
  3. Top with a generous spoonful of the tropical salsa.

Final Touch

  1. Optional: Garnish with additional cilantro and a lime wedge before serving.
