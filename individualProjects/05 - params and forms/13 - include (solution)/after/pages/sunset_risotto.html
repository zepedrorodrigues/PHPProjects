Sunset Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth, warmed
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon saffron threads
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish

Instructions:

Prepare the Base

  1. Heat olive oil in a large, wide pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add Arborio rice to the pan and stir to coat in the oil, cook for 2-3 minutes.

Saffron Infusion

  1. In a separate bowl, steep the saffron threads in a cup of warm broth for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Add the saffron-infused broth to the rice, stirring continuously until the liquid is absorbed.

Cooking the Risotto

  1. Begin adding the remaining warm broth one cup at a time, stirring frequently and waiting for the liquid to be absorbed before adding more.
  2. After the first two cups of broth have been absorbed, add the dry white wine. Continue stirring.

Add Color and Flavor

  1. Add the halved cherry tomatoes into the mix for color and a slight tartness.
  2. Once the rice is cooked to al dente and has a creamy consistency, remove from heat.

Final Touches

  1. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and butter for richness.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley.
