Citrus Symphony Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1 orange, thinly sliced
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 1 lime, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

Prepare the Marinade

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, honey, salt, pepper, and paprika.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Prepare the Oven and Pan

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Assemble the Dish

  1. Place the marinated salmon fillets on the baking sheet.
  2. Arrange the orange, lemon, and lime slices on top of each fillet.

Bake and Serve

  1. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until salmon is cooked through.
  2. Garnish with fresh dill before serving.
